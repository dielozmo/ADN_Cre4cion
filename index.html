Present & Future of construction

We team up with organizations that seek greatness and value in their construction projects, in a world saturated with normality.

what we do

We help organization linked to the construction sector to optimize and boost their proposal to the market, through process improvement.

We inject innovation in it's work structure supported by digital adoption and BIM (building information modeling) methodology and practical tools.

Services

Our propose is to improve our partner's project, innovating in four strategic areas:

Coordinate

Based on BIM Methodology, we work as the core of the project, cluster all the areas involve, since the design to the build.

3D Modeling

A digital 3D model is made up of all the real elementos that will make up the building with the information of each element, using Revit.

Family Creation

Digitalization of elements that make up the project. Each element with information: size, material type, color, physic properties, chemical properties

Digital Transformation

We help construction companies to get in the digital world by tools and change in culture and processes.
